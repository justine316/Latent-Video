Welcome, seeker. Fidelis Academy is not merely a school — it is a community of faith, learning, and virtue, united across distances by our shared Catholic identity.

⚜  Our Mission

Fidelis Academy provides an authentically Catholic education to families across the world through a decentralized model that honors the dignity of each family as the primary educator of their children.

⚜  Why Fidelis Academy?

✝

Faith First

Every subject is taught in the light of Catholic truth. Faith is not an add-on — it is the integrating center of all knowledge.

🌐

Decentralized

Our distributed model means families across the country and world can participate. No boundaries, no borders — one community in Christ.

👪

Community

Despite being distributed, our students form deep bonds through virtual community, live sessions, retreats, and pilgrimages.

⚜  Admissions Process

  1. Submit Inquiry — Complete the form below to request information.
  2. Application — Receive and complete the formal application packet.
  3. Interview — Family interview with our admissions director (virtual available).
  4. Acceptance — Receive your acceptance letter and enrollment packet.
  5. Enrollment — Submit enrollment forms and initial tuition deposit.
