Formatio Fidei

Faith Formation

At Fidelis Academy, faith is not a subject — it is the foundation of all learning. Our formation program integrates Catholic teaching into every aspect of student life.

🕯

Daily Prayer

Each school day begins and ends in prayer. Morning offering, Angelus at noon, and evening prayer are central rhythms of our community life.

  • Morning Offering at 8:00 AM
  • Angelus at 12:00 PM
  • Evening Prayer at 3:00 PM
  • Rosary on Fridays
✝

Sacramental Life

We encourage regular participation in the Sacraments as the primary means of grace and spiritual growth.

  • Weekly Mass on Wednesdays
  • Monthly Confession available
  • Feast day celebrations
  • Advent & Lenten programs
🤝

Service & Charity

Faith without works is dead. Our students engage in acts of corporal and spiritual mercy throughout the year.

  • Community service hours required
  • Food bank partnerships
  • Prison ministry visits
  • Annual mission trip
⛺

Retreats

Annual retreats provide students with time for silence, reflection, and deepening their personal relationship with God.

  • Freshmen: Encounter Retreat
  • Sophomores: Discernment Retreat
  • Juniors: Ignatian Silent Retreat
  • Seniors: Graduation Retreat
