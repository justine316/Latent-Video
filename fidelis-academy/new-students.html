WHY FIDELIS

The Fidelis Difference

CLASSICAL CURRICULUM

Rigorous academics grounded in the Western canon — Latin, rhetoric, mathematics, sciences and the arts. Our students leave equipped to excel at the world's finest universities.

CHARACTER FORMATION

Every lesson, every activity and every tradition at Fidelis is designed to cultivate virtue: courage, integrity, temperance, and an unflinching sense of duty.

PILGRIMAGE PROGRAMME

Our acclaimed pilgrimage journeys take students to the great historical and spiritual sites of Britain and Europe — learning beyond the classroom walls.

ELITE ATHLETICS

A full programme of traditional sports — rowing, rugby, fencing, cricket and cross-country — building discipline, teamwork and competitive excellence.

ARTS & CULTURE

Music, drama, debate and fine arts are core to life at Fidelis. We believe the cultivated mind is the foundation of a life well lived.

LIFE-LONG COMMUNITY

Join a global network of Fidelis alumni spanning five continents. The bonds forged here last a lifetime — in friendship, faith and shared purpose.
